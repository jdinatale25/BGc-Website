About Blueprint Growth Marketing

A Fort Worth marketing company built by someone who came from data and finance — not advertising.

Our Story

Blueprint Growth Marketing grew out of a genuine interest in how business systems work and what's possible when automation is set up correctly. Jake has spent his career building data pipelines and automated workflows, and that same mindset shapes how he approaches marketing.

The tools that help larger organizations run well, like automated communication, structured reporting, and data-backed decision-making, work just as well for a local service business. Blueprint Growth Marketing exists to build those systems for Fort Worth local businesses.

No long-term contracts. No surprise fees. If it's not working, you walk. That's the deal.

The Founder

Jake DiNatale

Founder, Blueprint Growth Marketing

Jake grew up in Boston and moved to Fort Worth in 2018 to attend TCU, where he graduated from AddRan College of Liberal Arts with a degree in Economics. He's spent the years since building data pipelines, automating financial workflows, and delivering analytics for some of the largest financial institutions in Texas.

He started Blueprint Growth Marketing to take that same thinking and apply it somewhere it was clearly missing: helping Fort Worth local businesses grow online without paying agency prices to do it.

Outside of work, Jake is a Brazilian jiu-jitsu practitioner and coach. It's a discipline that, not unlike building a business, rewards patience, consistency, and knowing when to apply pressure.

Our Values

These three principles guide everything we do at Blueprint Growth Marketing.

You Work With Jake, Not an Account Manager

At a traditional agency, the person who sold you the contract is never the person doing the work. Here, Jake handles the strategy, the execution, and the reporting. You have direct access to the person who actually knows your business.

Built on Systems, Not Gut Feelings

Most marketing decisions get made based on what looks good or what someone read in a blog post. Jake's background is in data pipelines and financial analytics — every recommendation we make is based on what the numbers show, not what sounds right.

No Contracts, No Surprises

We don't lock you in because we don't think we need to. If the work isn't producing results, you should be able to leave — and we should have to earn your business every month. That's how we structure every engagement.
