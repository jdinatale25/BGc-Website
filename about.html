About Blueprint Growth Marketing

Our Story

Blueprint Growth Marketing is the product of a genuine love for technology, business systems, and what automation can do when it's pointed at the right problem. Jake has spent his career building data pipelines and automated workflows — and that same obsession with efficient, scalable systems is what drives how he approaches marketing.

The tools that help large organizations operate with precision — automated communication, structured reporting, data-backed decision-making — are just as powerful for a local service business. Blueprint Growth Marketing exists to build those systems for Fort Worth small businesses.

No long-term contracts. No surprise fees. If it's not working, you walk. That's the deal.

The Founder

Jake DiNatale

Founder, Blueprint Growth Marketing

Jake grew up in Boston and moved to Fort Worth in 2018 to attend TCU, where he graduated from AddRan College of Liberal Arts with a degree in Economics. He's spent the years since building data pipelines, automating financial workflows, and delivering analytics for some of the largest financial institutions in Texas.

He started Blueprint Growth Marketing to take that same systematic, results-driven approach and apply it to a different problem — helping Fort Worth small businesses grow online without paying enterprise agency prices.

Outside of work, Jake is a Brazilian jiu-jitsu practitioner and coach — a discipline that, not unlike building a business, rewards patience, consistency, and knowing when to apply pressure.
