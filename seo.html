What You Get

Local SEO That Drives Real Traffic

Search Engine Optimization — what most people call "local SEO" — is the work of making sure your business shows up when someone nearby searches for what you offer. It's not a one-time fix. The work you do in month one makes month six easier. We manage it consistently so the results build over time.

Google Business Profile Management

Your Google Business Profile is usually the first thing someone sees before they decide to call. We keep it complete, accurate, and active: posts, photo updates, Q&A responses, and review monitoring, so Google has a reason to keep showing you.

Website Metadata Optimization

Title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, and schema markup — we optimize and maintain the on-page signals that tell Google exactly what your business does and where you serve.

Local Competitor Analysis

We track what your Fort Worth competitors are ranking for, where they have gaps, and where you have opportunities to capture search traffic they're leaving on the table. Your strategy is built around your specific market.

Monthly Reporting

Each month you get a report on ranking movement, profile performance, and traffic. Where you rank, how often your listing comes up, and whether the numbers are moving. Nothing inflated.

Local Citation & Consistency

Your business name, address, and phone number need to be consistent across every directory that influences local rankings. We audit and correct inconsistencies that quietly drag your rankings down.

Ongoing Strategy Adjustments

Rankings shift. New competitors show up. Google changes things. We track it monthly and adjust so you're not left reacting to something that changed three months ago.
